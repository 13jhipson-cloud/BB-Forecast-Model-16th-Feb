# Why the `ScaledCohortAvg` row flipped variance positive

Your test result makes sense. `ScaledCohortAvg` is not a direct "set the month rate" control:

- It scales a rolling cohort average (lookback window), not a single fixed month rate.
- If the underlying rolling average in MOB 4/5 differs from the backtest monthâ€™s required rate, a single scale factor can over-correct one month.
- So a ratio fitted on Oct+Nov total can still produce a positive variance in each month (or vice versa).
